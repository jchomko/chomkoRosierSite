Chomko & Rosier is a design studio creating interesting public projects with technology. Contact us here

Founded by Matthew Rosier and Jonathan Chomko, the studio focuses on non-screen-based interactive work. We follow a site-specific and user-centred design process, often incorporating new technology. Our work has been recognized by the London Design Museum, and has been exhibited at the Sydney Opera House, Mobile Museum Hong Kong, Perugia Journalism Festival, and Nuit Blanche Montreal.
We're interested in discussing comissions for non-screen based interactive work, and are available for consulting and workshops.
