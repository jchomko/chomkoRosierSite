Chomko & Rosier is a design studio focused on creating novel, human-centred applications of technology that can empower users and enhance our experience of the environment around us. Contact us at info@chomkorosier.com.

Founded by Matthew Rosier and Jonathan Chomko with backgrounds in architecture and interaction design respectively, the studio explores embodied applications of technology, following a site-specific and user-centred design process. Our interrogation of the brief and context allows us to develop an appropriate response; whether a public installation, interactive experience or product.

Our work has been nominated for Design of Year by the London Design Museum, and has been exhibited at the Sydney Opera House, Mobile Museum Hong Kong, the Perugia Journalism Festival, and Nuit Blanche Montreal. In addition to full design services, we offer consultancy and workshops.
